# Incident Response Guide

## Escalation Policy

Severity-one incidents must be escalated to the duty manager within 10 minutes of declaration.

## Response Steps

1. Assign an incident commander and begin evidence capture.
2. Notify the duty manager within 10 minutes of declaration.
3. Publish the first customer communication.

## After-Hours Incidents

After-hours severity-one incidents must be escalated to the duty manager within 10 minutes of declaration. The on-call system must still page an incident commander immediately.
