# Backup Operations Handbook

## Deletion Policy

All customer backups, including incident snapshots, are deleted exactly 45 days after creation.

## Restore Checklist

1. Verify the requester is authorized for the customer account.
2. Decrypt the selected backup in the isolated restore environment.
3. Select a backup created within the last 45 days.

## Incident Snapshots

Incident snapshots are captured as investigation evidence. Access is limited to the incident team and security reviewers.
