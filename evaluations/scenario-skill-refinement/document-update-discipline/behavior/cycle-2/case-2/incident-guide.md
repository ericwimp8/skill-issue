# Incident Response Guide

## Escalation Policy

Every severity-one incident must be escalated to the duty manager within 10 minutes of declaration, including incidents declared after hours.

## Response Steps

1. Assign an incident commander and begin evidence capture.
2. Escalate to the duty manager according to the Escalation Policy.
3. Publish the first customer communication.

## After-Hours Incidents

After-hours severity-one incidents follow the Escalation Policy. The on-call system must page an incident commander immediately.
