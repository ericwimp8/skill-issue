# Customer Export Guide

## Retention Policy

All customer exports, including diagnostic exports, remain available for exactly 30 days after creation.

## Export Procedure

1. Confirm the requester has export access.
2. Generate the encrypted archive.
3. Download the archive within 30 days.

## Diagnostic Exports

Diagnostic exports support investigations of recurring issues and remain available for exactly 30 days. They use the same encryption and access controls as customer exports.
