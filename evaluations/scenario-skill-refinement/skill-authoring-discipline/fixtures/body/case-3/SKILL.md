# Incident Handoff Review

- Determine whether the next responder can continue safely without rediscovering critical context.
- Identify missing, ambiguous, stale, or conflicting details that could change response decisions.
- Distinguish confirmed facts from hypotheses and unsupported conclusions.
- Prioritize operational gaps, then recommend focused corrections or follow-up questions.
