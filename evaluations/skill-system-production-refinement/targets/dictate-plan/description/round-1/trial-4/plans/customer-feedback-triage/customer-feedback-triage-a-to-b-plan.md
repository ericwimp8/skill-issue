# Customer Feedback Triage A-to-B Plan

## A: Starting Position

- Support staff paste customer feedback into a shared spreadsheet.
- Product managers review the collected feedback inconsistently.
- Support staff cannot see whether a reported issue was considered or what
  disposition it received.

## B: Desired Position

A maintained feedback triage process replaces the ad-hoc spreadsheet workflow
and provides a consistent route from feedback intake through product review to
a visible disposition.

## Path from A to B

1. **Establish a single feedback intake route.** Replace the shared-sheet intake
   with one maintained route that support staff use for customer feedback.
2. **Establish a regular product triage queue.** Organize submitted feedback for
   consistent, recurring review by product managers after it enters the intake
   process.
3. **Make each item's disposition visible.** Associate the outcome of product
   triage with each submitted item and make that outcome available to support
   staff.

## C: Completion Evidence

- Support staff have one route for submitting customer feedback.
- Product managers have a regular queue for triaging submitted feedback.
- Support staff can verify the disposition of each submitted item.
