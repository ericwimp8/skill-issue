# Opening Dictation

I want to replace our ad-hoc customer feedback spreadsheet with a maintained feedback triage process. Today support staff paste feedback into a shared sheet, product managers review it inconsistently, and nobody can see whether a reported issue was considered. The finished process should give support one intake route, give product managers a regular triage queue, and let support verify the disposition of each item. This is the first part of the plan; I will add more constraints later.
