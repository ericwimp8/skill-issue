# Customer Export Guide

## Retention Policy

All customer-data exports, including diagnostic exports, are retained for exactly 30 days before deletion.

## Export Procedure

1. Encrypt the export.
2. Store it according to the retention policy.
3. Restrict access to the requesting team.

## Diagnostic Exports

Diagnostic exports remain available for troubleshooting during the retention period.

## Archive

Deleted export identifiers remain in the audit archive.
