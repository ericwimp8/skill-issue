# Service Account Guide

## Credential Rotation

Privileged service accounts rotate credentials every 120 days.

## Onboarding Checklist

1. Create the account.
2. Record a 120-day rotation reminder.
3. Limit access to the owning team.

## Break-Glass Accounts

Break-glass accounts do not require scheduled credential rotation.

## Audit Records

Rotation events are retained for one year.
