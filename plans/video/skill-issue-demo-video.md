# Skill Issue Demo Video

## Opening

Start on a dark screen with a blinking terminal cursor. Type `skill issue`, then let the words **Skill Issue** expand into the centre with a short, soft spring animation. Bring in the hook underneath:

> It’s not always a skill problem, but it’s always a Skill Issue.

Keep the opening simple, familiar, and quick. Move into the product within the first six seconds.

## Core Story

1. Skills are valuable, but failures are difficult to diagnose.
2. Skill, model, and harness failures often look the same.
3. Skill Issue helps create, evaluate, and refine skills from ordinary-language requests.
4. Skill Issue also measures whether a model-and-harness environment invokes skills when expected.
5. Published benchmarks provide a starting point; the open-source CLI lets people evaluate combinations that have not been covered yet.
6. Codex and GPT-5.6 were used to build the project, including skills that helped build and evaluate other skills.

## Claims to Replace With Final Evidence

- Use only real model-and-harness results produced by the completed evaluation campaign.
- Show only CLI commands, downloads, and workflows that exist in the released product.
- Describe observed calls and misses without claiming universal reliability, winners, or guarantees.
