# Privacy Audit

- Durable project paths are repository-relative.
- `workflow-prompt.md` uses `<repo-root>` and contains no machine-specific checkout path.
- Skill, plans, fixtures, reports, records, and audits contain no personal or business identity, username, credential, secret value, or private service endpoint.
- Native session evidence is referenced by session identifier and `~/.codex/sessions/2026/07/21/`; no host username or absolute home path is retained in campaign documents.
- Synthetic credential strings and service names are explicitly fixture-owned and do not represent live access.
