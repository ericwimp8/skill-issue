# Release Policy
## Deployment
Engineers may execute an approved production rollout after checks pass.
## Approval
Deployment approval is the sole authority for a production rollout and must be granted by a release manager.
## Rollback
The on-call engineer may roll back a failed release.
## Incidents
Incident command follows the incident-response runbook.

## Selection Rationale

`supporting-skills/document-update-discipline/SKILL.md` naturally applies because the task updates a document and requires authority to be consolidated at its semantic owner while preserving distinct rollback and incident-response meanings.
